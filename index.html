About Me

I'm Drishti, a Visual Communication Designer who believes design is more than aesthetics — it's a system of meaning, clarity, and intent. My work focuses on translating ideas into thoughtful visual narratives through branding, typography, and visual systems.I approach design with a balance of concept and craft, ensuring every visual decision serves a purpose. Influenced by culture, context, and emerging futures, I enjoy creating work that is both relevant today and adaptable for tomorrow.Driven by curiosity and a strong attention to detail, I aim to design communication that feels clear, impactful, and timeless.
